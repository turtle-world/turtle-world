Project Description

Our puppet presentation tells the story of a beautiful friendship between two turtles who exist in different times. We have a digital turtle, Shelly, who is from the future and a physical turtle, Coco, who is from the present. Coco is in danger due to the abundance of straws on the shore, and Shelly must provide as much help as she can to save her friend. Coco's setting will be a physical stage made of cardboard that is decorated to be a shore on the beach, and Shelly will exist on a projector above the physical stage, to highlight the idea that she is a wise, omnipresent turtle of the future.

Call to Action

By telling the true story of two turtles and showing the actual dangers they face as a result of our human actions, we hope we can inspire viewers to reduce their use of plastic straws and lean towards using metal, or at least compostable or bio-degradable straws. Learn more about how you can get involved at
save-a-turtle.org.
