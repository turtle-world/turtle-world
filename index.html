Project Description

Our puppet presentation tells the story of a beautiful friendship between two turtles who exist in different times. One turtle is from the future, and the other is from the present. The turtle of the present is in danger, and the turtle of the future must work to save their friend. The turtles also exist in different realms, with the turtle of the present existing in our own physical world, and the turtle of the future existing in the digital world.

Call to Action

By telling the true story of two turtles and showing the actual dangers they face as a result of our human actions, we hope we can inspire viewers to reduce their use of plastic straws and lean towards using metal, or at least compostable or bio-degradable straws. Learn more about how you can get involved at
save-a-turtle.org.
